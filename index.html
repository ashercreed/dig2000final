New Technology Challenges

Games

Gaming can be not only enaging and entertaining but also beneficial. Research shows that playing video games can increase cognitive skills (ie. reasoning, perception, etc.) as well as social skills. With online multiplayer, gamers can connect with people across the globe from the comfort of their own home all while training their brain with hand-eye coordination. It's equally excercising your social skills and physical/psychological ones. Depending on the game, different results can occur. Puzzles games test your brain's reasoning more than FPS ones which help your strategy skills. Gaming can also help relax you and help blow off steam, from a calming explorative game to a high-action shooter. In general, gaming is quite good for you and others.

Deepfakes

Deepfakes are realtively easy to make, and while the public uses them for a laugh, they are serious threats to the government and people's privacy. From defaming people by deepfaking porn and other explicit acts to creating artificial presidental speeches and threats, deepfakes are literally putting words into someone's mouth and forcing them to do things they have yet to do. They also are messing with AI and other facial recognition technology, whether that's good or bad. All in all, people's lives are being ruined by this simple yet effective ability to put one's face on another's and it is certainly no laughing matter.

AR/VR

Augmented Reality (AR) and Virtual Reality (VR) technology is making its way into the public rapidly. While mainly being used for gaming, this technology has the potential to be used for anything from modeling and testing without using real life materials to hands-on job training like preparing and teaching future surgeons. This is equally less wasteful for physical materials and more money-saving in the same vein. AR and VR can help others experience what they never could have thought possible and try new things without harm in a virtual space. I look forward to what the future holds for this new and upcoming technology.
